Real Estate And Property

Our Core Services

  • Real Estate & RERA Law
  • Title Search & Due Diligence

Real estate transactions involve significant financial commitments and legal complexity. Our Real Estate & RERA practice provides comprehensive support for property development, purchase, sale, leasing, regulatory compliance, and dispute resolution. We ensure that every transaction is legally sound, transparent, and compliant with statutory requirements.

Our Real Estate & RERA Expertise

We advise builders, developers, investors, financial institutions, and individual property owners on all aspects of real estate law. Our services combine legal due diligence, documentation, and regulatory assistance to protect our clients’ interests.

Property Transactions & Documentation

Handle property transactions in Ahmedabad with expert sale deeds, purchase/lease agreements, joint development agreements, and builder-buyer contracts.

RERA Registration & Compliance

Ensure RERA compliance in Ahmedabad via project registrations, advisory for promoters/agents/homebuyers, and audits/regulatory filings.

Real Estate Dispute Resolution

Resolve real estate disputes in Ahmedabad including builder-buyer issues, delayed possession claims, and RERA complaints/appeals/litigation.

Advisory for Developers & Investors

Provide developer/investor advisory in Ahmedabad on project structuring, risk assessment/financial compliance, and land acquisition support.

Conducting a thorough title search and legal due diligence is essential before purchasing or investing in property. Our team ensures that clients receive a complete, verified, and legally accurate assessment of property ownership, history, encumbrances, and compliance before entering into any transaction.

Our Due Diligence Expertise

We perform detailed document reviews, field verifications, and risk assessments to help clients avoid disputes, fraud, and financial loss. Our due diligence reports provide clear, actionable insights to support informed decision-making.

Title Verification & Ownership Checks

Ensure clean property ownership with thorough historical title verification, encumbrance and lien checks, and clear identification of all legal heirs and successors for risk-free transactions.

Property Compliance & Approvals

Secure fully compliant properties through verification of approvals and sanctions, zoning and building regulation checks, plus RERA and municipal authority clearances.

Risk Assessment & Reporting

Make informed decisions with detailed due diligence reports that highlight legal defects, potential disputes, and practical risk-mitigation recommendations.

Support for Buyers, Sellers & Investors

Get end-to-end support in negotiations, documentation checks for banks and financial institutions, and specialised advisory for domestic and NRI property investments.
