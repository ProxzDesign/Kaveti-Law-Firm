You asked. We answered

But if we didn’t. Just reach out us and we’ll be happy to answer your question.

What types of legal services do you offer internationally?

We provide a full suite of global legal services including business formation, contract drafting, dispute resolution, and intellectual property protection.

Do you handle both personal and business cases?

Yes, our firm assists clients with both personal matters—such as family law, immigration, and estate planning—and business cases, including corporate law, contracts, and regulatory compliance.

How do you manage cases across different countries?

We collaborate with a network of trusted international partners and local experts to ensure your case is managed seamlessly across jurisdictions. Our approach includes direct communication, careful research, and tailored legal strategies for every region involved.

Can I consult your team online or remotely?

Absolutely. We offer consultations via video call, phone, and email to make it easy for you to contact us from anywhere. All case updates and meetings can be handled remotely for your convenience.

What are your consultation fees?

Our initial consultation fees are competitive and clearly communicated upfront. The exact fee depends on the type and complexity of your case. Please contact us for a personalized quote or to discuss your needs.

How long does it take to resolve an international case?

The timeline varies depending on the legal matter, countries involved, and documentation required. After an initial review, we’ll provide you with an estimated timeframe and keep you updated throughout each stage of the process.

Do you assist with immigration or visa issues?

Yes, we provide guidance and legal support for immigration matters—including visa applications, work permits, residency requests, and cross-border compliance. Our team can advise you on the best options for your specific situation.
