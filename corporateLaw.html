Corporate law governs the formation, management, and operations of businesses. We provide comprehensive advisory and compliance support to companies across all stages of their lifecycle—from incorporation to expansion and restructuring.

Our Corporate Law Expertise

We work with startups, SMEs, and large corporations to ensure their business activities align with statutory requirements and industry best practices.

Business Formation & Structuring

Establish businesses in Ahmedabad with expert company/LLP incorporation, shareholding/equity planning, and joint venture/partnership setups.

Corporate Governance & Compliance

Ensure governance in Ahmedabad via board advisory/frameworks, ROC filings/statutory compliance, and secretarial audit support.

Corporate Advisory & Strategy

Drive strategy in Ahmedabad with investment/financing guidance, regulatory approvals/licensing, and comprehensive legal risk assessments.

Corporate Disputes & Resolution

Resolve disputes in Ahmedabad including shareholder conflicts, oppression/mismanagement cases, and full corporate litigation representation.

International law governs cross-border transactions, global trade, regulatory compliance, and disputes involving multiple jurisdictions. Our team assists businesses, investors, and individuals engaged in international operations.

Our International Law Expertise

We help clients navigate treaties, regulatory frameworks, foreign business operations, and multinational transactions.

Cross-Border Transactions

Support for complex cross-border transactions, including international joint ventures, global mergers and acquisitions, and robust licensing and franchising structures that protect your commercial interests while aligning with multi-jurisdictional regulations and business goals.

International Regulatory Compliance

Guidance on global compliance frameworks covering anti-corruption and anti-bribery standards, sanctions and export controls, trade regulations, and international tax considerations to minimise legal risk in multi-country operations.

International Dispute Resolution

End-to-end support in resolving international disputes through arbitration and mediation, enforcement of foreign judgments, and handling cross-border commercial conflicts to protect your rights and commercial relationships worldwide.

Advisory for Global Expansion

Strategic advisory for global expansion, including market-entry strategies, foreign subsidiary formation, and tailored international contract drafting to enable smooth, compliant, and sustainable growth in new jurisdictions.

M&A transactions require strategic planning, financial insight, and robust legal due diligence. We assist companies in successfully executing acquisitions, mergers, divestitures, and strategic alliances.

Our M&A Expertise

Our team supports clients through all stages—from deal evaluation to negotiation and post-transaction integration.

Transaction Structuring & Strategy

Comprehensive support in designing optimal deal structures, including share and asset purchases, joint ventures, and strategic alliances, backed by valuation insights and financial modeling guidance to align commercial and legal objectives.

Legal Due Diligence

Thorough legal due diligence covering corporate records, key contracts, assets, liabilities, and regulatory compliance to uncover risks, validate assumptions, and protect value before signing or closing.

Transaction Documentation

End-to-end drafting and negotiation of core deal documents, including Share Purchase Agreements, Shareholders’ Agreements, and Business Transfer Agreements, tailored to allocate risk clearly and safeguard all parties’ rights.

Closing & Post-Merger Integration

Support through regulatory approvals, conditions precedent, and closing formalities, followed by guidance on post-merger integration and corporate restructuring to ensure smooth transition and realization of deal synergies.

Contract law governs agreements between parties and forms the backbone of commercial operations. We draft, review, and negotiate contracts that protect business interests while ensuring legal clarity.

Our Contract Law Expertise

We advise companies across industries to minimize risk and maintain strong commercial relationships.

Drafting & Reviewing Commercial Contracts

End-to-end drafting and review of service agreements, vendor contracts, employment contracts, and partnership or joint venture agreements, focused on clear risk allocation, enforceability, and alignment with your commercial objectives.

Negotiation & Risk Advisory

Strategic negotiation of terms and conditions, including indemnities and limitation of liability, with contract lifecycle oversight to minimise disputes and protect long-term business interests.​

Specialized Business Agreements

Preparation of specialised commercial agreements such as licensing and technology contracts, franchise and distribution arrangements, and robust confidentiality and non-compete agreements tailored to Indian law.

Contract Disputes & Enforcement

Support in handling breach of contract claims through negotiation, arbitration, or litigation, and enforcing contractual rights efficiently while preserving key business relationships wherever possible.

Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) involves global investment flows into domestic markets and requires strict compliance with regulatory frameworks. We guide foreign investors and businesses entering India through all legal and procedural requirements.

Our FDI Expertise

Our team supports multinational companies, startups, and investors with approvals, structuring, and regulatory compliance.

Entry Strategy & Structuring

End-to-end support for foreign entry into India, including choice of wholly owned subsidiaries or joint ventures, evaluation of investment models, sector-specific FDI rules, and full FEMA/RBI compliance for a legally robust structure.

Regulatory Approvals & Documentation

Assistance with filings and approvals before RBI, DPIIT and other regulators, ensuring compliant share transfers, capital infusions, and efficient setup of liaison or project offices for foreign businesses.

Cross-Border Transactions

Advisory on inbound and outbound investments, foreign shareholding structures, and practical repatriation and exit strategies aligned with FEMA, sectoral caps, and tax considerations.

Ongoing Compliance & Support

Ongoing FEMA and FDI compliance through periodic reporting, sectoral cap and restriction advisory, and audit-ready documentation support to keep cross-border investments fully regulator-ready.
